Just like calories, the amount of carbohydrates in rice also differ depending on the type of rice. White rice nutritional value would include 90% carbohydrate, 8% protein and 2% fat.

How to cook brown rice?

Rinse the rice Put water on the stove and bring it to a boil Once the water is boiling add the brown rice Reduce heat to low and allow the rice to simmer until the rice is tender and most of the liquid has been absorbed. It should take about 40 to 50 minutes for the rice to cook.

How to cook basmati rice?

Add 1 cup of Basmati rice to a bowl of water Cover and allow it to soak for 20 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water then add 2 cups of water to one cup of rice on a pan. Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for 10-12 minutes. Remove lid and let the rice stand for 5 minutes before serving.

How to cook rice?

Rinse rice until water is clear. Boil water in a pot. Add rice and salt to the boiling water and let it simmer for 18 to 20 minutes. Once done let it rest off the heat for 10 minutes and then fluff.

What does rice contain?

White rice has about 90% carbohydrate, 8% protein and 2% fat.

What nutrients does rice contain?

Rice is a good source of magnesium, phosphorus, manganese, selenium, iron, folic acid, thiamine and niacin.
